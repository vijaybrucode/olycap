Important Message

Thank you for visiting the CAA of South Alabama Online Appointment Scheduler to schedule an appointment for assistance. CAAofSA operates various utility assistance programs, including LIHEAP Crisis and LIHEAP Subsidy.

Applications for assistance will be accepted by appointment only and are dependent on the availability of funds. Applicants must be a resident of Baldwin, Escambia, Clark, Monroe, Conecuh, Wilcox, or Marengo County. To receive assistance, you must provide the required documentation for and meet the income guidelines for each specific program.

On the day of your appointment, please arrive at the center a few minutes before your appointment time. When you arrive, call the main number of that center and a staff member will come outside to greet you and let you inside to begin processing your application.

List of Common Docs Required

Failure to provide all required documentation will delay your assistance.

  1. Social Security card(s) or official documentation with Social Security numbers (or Permanent Resident/Green Card) for everyone living in the household.
  2. Most recent utility bill, which could include any disconnect or past due notices, or verification from your landlord that heating expenses are included in your rent. You may also bring an eviction notice, if utilities are included in your monthly rent. Please note that a disconnect, past due or eviction notice is not always required; our staff will conduct a full assessment of your household's needs and match them to the services that are available at the time of your appointment. If you are applying for wood, coal, propane or kerosene, you need to bring a utility bill to verify your service address. If you participate in the Pre-Pay Electric Program, please present the statement from the utility company and include the account number, address and name on the account for all heating fuel sources and electric. If you do not have electric service, you must bring a piece of unopened first-class mail to verify your service address
  3. Proof of household income from the previous month or proof of zero income; for example:
    1. Check stubs (MUST have all check stubs showing pay dates and gross income for the calendar month prior to your appointment date and verification that you did not receive the maximum number of checks issued by your employer)
    2. OR, Current SSI/Social Security Award letter or Current Food Stamp Award letter
    3. OR, Unemployment Check Stubs
  4. Proof of ID
  5. Examples of items which cannot be accepted are previous LIHEAP applications, bank statements, copies of benefit checks, and 1099 tax forms. We cannot accept K-TAP letters or Medical Coverage Letters as proof of other income.
